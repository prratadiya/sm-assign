Veridic Technologies

Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration & Excellence!

Since 2010, Veridic Technologies has been developing solutions for Telecom Operators, Ecommerce, Online Marketplaces, Mobile Buyback Providers, Distributors, Wholesalers, Phone Recyclers & Warehouse providers across the globe.

Started in 2010 by Mr. Ankur Thakur as an outsourcing firm for Brightstar Corporation (a USD 10 Billion company), now Veridic has risen to become an internationally known brand having 150+ employees operating from three offices the U.S, UK & India.
