Company Technologies

Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration & Excellence!

Since 2010, Company Technologies has been developing solutions for Telecom Operators, Ecommerce, Online Marketplaces, Mobile Buyback Providers, Distributors, Wholesalers, Phone Recyclers & Warehouse providers across the globe.

Started in 2010 by Mr. Ankur Thakur as an outsourcing firm for Brightstar Corporation (a USD 10 Billion company), now Company has risen to become an internationally known brand having 150+ employees operating from three offices the U.S, UK & India.

Our mission and vision

To Build a World-Class Product Engineering & Design Consultancy
for the Best Ideas, Bringing Innovation & Disruption in Industry

Driving value for our clients and partners

Company Technologies works across the entire life cycle of a problem & supports our brand partners through the unique idea curation, design, MVP, product strategy, engineering, deployment, maintenance & support.

We have expert resources in both open source & licensed web technologies, mobile apps, UI/UX design, testing, cloud, infrastructure & marketing to support our clients through innovations & growth.

Our software & systems are running across Apple stores across the US and Europe. These aren’t any obscure components but full-fledged systems used by Apple and their end customers in Apple stores on regular basis.

More than 98% of Company’s clients acclaim our delivery model, reliability, passion, creativity, and unique ability to handle widest range of their IT needs from design, development, marketing to cloud & infrastructure set up, server management, maintenance & support. Company is relentlessly working on the emerging and upcoming technologies to provide market leading solutions for our clients.

Our team

An exceptional team of professionals with a wealth of experience in technology, product engineering, design, development, testing & growth strategy.
